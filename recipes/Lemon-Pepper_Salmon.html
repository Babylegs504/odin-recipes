Lemon-Pepper Salmon

Description

This tasty dish of lean fish will be one to stun your guests and have them coming back for more!

It takes 15 minutes of prep, 8 minutes to cook, and serves 4!

Ingredients

  • 4(4 ounce) salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • lemon pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven broiler. Lightly grease baking sheet.
  2. Place salmon on the baking sheet. Mix the melted butter and soy sauce in a small bowl, and brush over the salmon. Sprinkle salmon with lemon pepper.
  3. Broil salmon 6 to 8 minutes, depending on thickness, or until easily flaked with a fork.
